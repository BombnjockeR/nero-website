Core Rules

  • MVP HP is increased by +100%, and MVP cards are balanced (modified).
  • PK is always ON on every MVP map.
  • Guild Alliances are disabled.
  • Emperium HP: 300,000.
  • Zeny is universal — shared across every character on the same account (no bank needed).
  • Skills use no gemstones.
  • Every kind of Box cannot drop from monsters.
  • Selling items to NPCs gives 50% of the normal price.
  • @autotrade (vending) is only allowed in Morroc.
  • Security: newest Gepard Shield.

PvP / WoE Notes

  • Assumptio is removed when entering a WoE room or castle.
  • Speed Potion is enabled in WoE (10-second delay).
  • All players auto-hide pets in WoE; pets are still allowed.
