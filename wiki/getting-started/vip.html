VIP Benefits

VIP is available from the Item Mall and various rewards (Battle Pass, events).

While VIP is active

  • +50% EXP, Job, and Drop rate
  • All dishes +3 (stat food bonus)
  • Storage limit +300
  • Permanent support buffs: Suffragium, Impositio, Assumptio, Spirit Link, Magnificat, Agility, Blessing

Tip: VIP time can be earned for free through the Battle Pass, Hourly rewards, and the Gacha Box.
