New Player Start

Everything a fresh account receives, plus the one-time buff you choose.

Starter Buff (choose 1)

Pick one after creating your character. It sits in a Costume ACC / Shadow slot, is account-bound, and can be upgraded later (a WoE castle item).

Option Effect
1 Reduce Damage from Demi-Human +1%
2 Reduce Damage from Non-Player +1%
3 Increase Damage to Demi-Human +1%
4 Increase Damage to Non-Player +1%
5 Increase Max HP & SP +1%

Starting Items (per account)

  • 50,000 zeny
  • 1x Main Gauche [3]
  • 1x Zipper Bear Card

Freebies (per account)

  • Battle Manual x2
  • HE Battle Manual x1
  • VIP 3 Days x1

All starter items are account-bound — they can't be traded or stored in Kafra.
