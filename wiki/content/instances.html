Instances & Dungeons

Instances reward Nero Dungeon Scrap, Common Dungeon Treasure, and tokens.

Dungeon C (example)

Completion rewards include: - 1x Common Dungeon Treasure - 1x Nero Dungeon Scrap

Spend Scrap and Treasure at the Reward Exchange for refine materials, gacha scrolls, and currencies.

Where the currencies go

  • Nero Dungeon Scrap → Dungeon Reward NPC (BSB, Era scrolls, HD ores, orbs, coins).
  • Common Dungeon Treasure → open for a random reward (see Gacha Boxes).
