Endless Tower

A climbing dungeon with saveable progress and scaling difficulty.

Rules

  • Every 10th floor, the party leader can save. If the party stays together, the NPC can recall members.
  • The leader picks a difficulty before climbing — harder = better rewards.
  • Multiple parties can enter at once (no queue).
  • Save points reset weekly.
  • Rewards are given automatically at set floors.

Difficulty modifiers

Rewards (every 10 floors)

Mode Reward per 10 floors
Normal Nero Dungeon Scrap x1, Nero Token, Nero Shadow Orb, Common Dungeon Treasure x1
Hard Scrap x2, Common Dungeon Treasure x2, tokens/orbs
Extreme Scrap x4, Nero Token x4, Common Dungeon Treasure x3

Floors 1–25 feature themed monster waves (every 5th floor is a mini-boss floor, e.g. Golden Thief Bug on F5, Mistress on F10, Phreeoni on F15, Drake on F20).
