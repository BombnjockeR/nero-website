Battle Pass

The Battle Pass replaces daily quests. 1 quest = 1 level (Kill + Collect objectives), limited to 1 per day. You can also buy a level with 10,000 CP (no limit if bought from the Item Mall). It resets monthly, and all rewards are account-bound.
