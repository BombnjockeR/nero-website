Mall Shops

The Mall has class-based NPCs selling weapons, ammo, and job materials at zeny prices.

Shops available

  • Swordman, Thief, Mage, Merchant, Acolyte, Archer, Taekwon/Ninja, Gunslinger
  • Ammunition (arrows, bullets, spheres, shuriken, kunai)
  • Creator Materials (potion/bottle-making ingredients)
  • Dye Shop (hair/cloth dyes, 300,000 zeny each)

Examples

Item Shop Price (zeny)
Two-Handed Sword Swordman 90,000
Damascus Thief 75,000
Arc Wand Mage 65,000
Emperium Anvil Merchant 5,000,000
Huuma Blaze Shuriken Ninja 120,000
Destroyer Gunslinger 250,000

The Mall covers standard gear only — custom/enchanted gear comes from gacha, Shadow, and Era systems.
