Economy & Vending

NeRO uses active zeny sinks and a WoE-linked vending tax to keep the economy healthy.

Zeny basics

  • Zeny is universal across your account.
  • Selling to NPCs returns only 50% of value.
  • Vending (@autotrade) is allowed only in Morroc.

Vending Tax

  • A 8% tax applies to vending sales.
  • The WoE-winning guild can claim 5% of vending tax as a reward (via Tax Redeem NPC).

Main zeny sinks

  • Refine attempts (5,000 zeny each; Shadow 200,000).
  • Shadow → Costume enchant (2,000,000 zeny).
  • NPC services, Item Mall zeny options, and gacha (zeny Era Relic).
