War of Emperium (WoE)

Key rules

  • Emperium HP: 300,000.
  • Guild cap in castles: 26 members.
  • Guild alliances: disabled.
  • Assumptio is removed inside WoE, and several skills are restricted (see Job Adjustments).
  • Speed Potion allowed (10s delay). All players auto-hide pets.

Winner Rewards

The winning guild claims: - Bloody Dead Branch x3 (guild leader) - Claim Vending Tax 5% (guild leader) — see Economy & Vending - Nero Coin x1 (all members) - Nero Shadow Orb x1 (all members)

LMS (Last Man Standing)

Runs 2–3 times a week near WoE times.
