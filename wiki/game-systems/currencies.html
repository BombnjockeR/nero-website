Currencies & Tokens

What each NeRO currency is for.

Currency Used for
Zeny Universal — shared across all characters on your account.
Cash Point (CP) Item Mall purchases. Earn 5 CP every 5 minutes online.
Nero Token (NT) Equipment option re-rolls.
Nero Shadow Orb (NSO) Buying & refining Shadow Equipment, and shadow option rolls.
Nero Coin General reward currency (exchanges, shops).
Nero Dungeon Scrap Instance/dungeon reward exchange currency.
Nero Premium Token Premium exchanges (Era Premium Headgear/Egg, Era Scroll Box).

How to earn NT & NSO

  • Battle Pass: total level 25 grants 15; every level after 25 grants +5.
  • Endless Tower, Instances, WoE, PvP/MVP, and events all reward them.

Passive earning

  • Hourly Point: 1 hour online (or AFK) = 1 hourly point → spend at the Hourly Point Shop.
  • Rest / Zen Area: only this zone gives its XP; +1.5% per hour for all levels.
