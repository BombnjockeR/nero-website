Shadow Equipment

Shadow gear grants powerful set bonuses. It can be traded.

Rules

  • Trade 10 Nero Shadow Orbs for a Shadow item of your choice.
  • Refine with 10 Nero Shadow Orbs + 200,000 zeny per attempt (see Refine System).
  • Bonuses scale with refine level; watch for the +7, +9, and set total-refine thresholds.
