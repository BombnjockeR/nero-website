Job Adjustments

Custom skill tweaks by class. Summary of the most important changes.

Lord Knight / Paladin

  • Concentration, Two-Hand Quicken, Aura Blade cannot be dispelled.
  • Spiral Pierce enabled with Two-Hand Sword (damage nerfed).
  • Bowling Bash — gutter-line removed, always deals double damage.
  • Pierce +40% damage; Brandish Spear +30%.
  • Two-Hand / Spear Quicken — add CRIT/FLEE per level; Holy Cross doubles with Two-Handed Spear.
  • Grand Cross cast time 3s → 2s, +10% damage.
  • Gospel disabled inside castle; Provoke disabled in WoE.

Whitesmith

  • Buffs cannot be dispelled under Soul Link.
  • Cart Termination and Mammonite zeny cost −50%.
  • Greed disabled.

Sniper

  • Sharp Shooting range 9 → 10 (splash stays 14).
  • Falcon Assault range 10.
  • Ankle Snare doesn't affect self/guild, limit 5, locks Body Relocation.
  • Land Mine / Blast Mine +300% damage; all traps ignore friendly/guild fire.

High Priest

  • Assumptio disabled/removed in WoE.
  • Kyrie Eleison fails against an Assumptio target.

Clown / Gypsy

  • Marionette Control disabled in GvG/WoE.
  • Tarot Card of Fate: Coma 3%, Dispel 7%.
  • All combo songs (Loki's, Drum, etc.) disabled in WoE.

High Wizard / Professor

  • Land Protector uses old behavior (blocks damage but doesn't remove AoE; edge cells have no effect).
  • Amplify Magic Power applies to first hit only.
  • Mind Breaker can re-apply; Ganbantein doesn't remove AoE. Abracadabra disabled.

Assassin / Stalker / Ninja

  • Sonic Blow −5% damage.
  • Throw Zeny range increased to 9.
  • Back Stab jumps behind target (or same cell if blocked); always triggers Double Attack.

Bio

  • Acid Terror +50% damage; Plant Cultivation disabled.

Guild Skills

  • All guild buff skills have 5x5 area; Emergency Call doesn't reset on disconnect.
