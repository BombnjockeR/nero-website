Skill Delays (Hard Delay)

NeRO uses a global hard delay of 100ms to prevent no-delay cheats and keep combat fair. Some skills have their own tuned delay:

Skill Delay
Asura Strike 1000ms
Arrow Shower 500ms
Sonic Blow 500ms
Magnum Break 450ms
Tarot Card of Fate 350ms
Body Relocation 310ms
Acid Demonstration 300ms
Lex Aeterna 250ms
Status Recovery 250ms
Ganbantein 220ms
Blitz Beat / Bowling Bash 220ms
Spiral Pierce / Sharp Shooting / Meteor Storm / Pressure / Lightning Crash 200ms
Soul Breaker 180ms
Kaupe / Kaite 175ms
Grimtooth / Storm Gust / Lord of Vermillion / Land Protector / Falcon Assault 150ms

This is a partial list of the most impactful skills. Delays are tuned for balanced PvP/WoE.
